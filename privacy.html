Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

In The Hat is a local party game. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or analytics
  • Usage data or crash reports

Local Storage

The app saves your current game state locally on your device using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.
